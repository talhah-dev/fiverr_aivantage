About Us

ChatGPT & CoPilot Training

Welcome to AiVantage, your partner in practical artificial intelligence training and consultancy. Our mission at AiVantage is straightforward: to safely close the gap between the potential of AI tools and their effective application in your organisation.

At AiVantage, we specialise in providing comprehensive training in widely available AI tools such as ChatGPT and Microsoft's Copilot. Our services cater to a wide range of organisations, ensuring that your team is well-equipped to harness these technologies for enhanced productivity and innovation.

Partnerships and Expertise

AiVantage was founded in September 2023 by Elliot Shimmin. As a solo-entrepreneur through 2022 & 2023, Elliot found enormous untapped value in using ChatGPT for operations, finance, compliance, marketing, strategy, ideation and much more. Recognising that many organisations were underutilising generative AI technologies, Elliot established AiVantage with the goal of maximising AI's impact in the business sector.

For the delivery of technical AI solutions, AiVantage has partnered with Sam Kay, an experienced software developer and fellow entrepreneur with experience in machine learning. This collaboration ensures that our clients receive a wider spectrum of AI solutions.

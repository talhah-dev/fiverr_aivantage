How it works:

Step 1 - Safety & Foundations:

Begin with a 1.5hr organisation-wide 'Basic ChatGPT Training', or 'Basic Copilot Training'. Live, interactive, and tailored to your organisation. Foster an AI growth mindset and learn the essential use cases, risks, limitations, mitigations, prompt-writing, and LLM mechanics.

We work on a 'pay on results' basis and our service is fully customisable.

Step 2 - Empowerment & Enhancement:

Learn to harness the full capabilities of ChatGPT or Copilot with 'Intermediate ChatGPT Training', or 'Intermediate CoPilot Training'. This is recommended only if your staff have access to ChatGPT Team or Enterprise, or Copilot for MS 365. Learn to get even more leverage from generative AI: build custom GPTs, use AI internet search, document uploads, and advanced prompt engineering advice.

Dedicated AI Guide and Trainer for your Organisation

Step 3 - Advanced Application & Customisation:

Further boost the productivity of select roles within your organisation. Your AiVantage Guide and Trainer will conduct a 'Workflow Audit' for each prioritised role, one by one, to identify opportunities for AI integration. They will then write, test, and refine bespoke prompts that can be deployed immediately and used routinely. Relevant staff receive training on those use cases.

Receive 4x bespoke training sessions per month.

Optional support:

If you're unsure about opting for a subscription to ChatGPT or Microsoft Copilot, or building a chatbot based on company knowledge, we can share our experience and advice. We offer strategy support, AI policy writing, compliance reviews, and technical solutions.
